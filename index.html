Background

I have spent the last several years involved in the concept of how software development, digital design, and innovative technologies are combined together. My dive into full-stack development has made me appreciate the vast affordances technology has to offer humankind.

As I entered this industry, I slowly but surely realized my real passion was a combination of robust backend architecture and intuitive frontend user interfaces. I find myself drawn recently to creating seamless user experiences and integrating those designs with powerful logic.

Philosophy

Humans are multifaceted, intersectional, and complex beings. Our technology should be the same way. Modern web development affords us the opportunity to bridge the gaps of current flat design and create a dynamic environment around us.

Conversely, we have the opportunity and the responsibility for that new technology to be flexible, scalable, and accessible by all humankind. Good code is not just about function; it's about building a sustainable foundation for the future.
